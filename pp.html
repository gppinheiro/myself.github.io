Home Automation

Explanation

Domotic is a term used in the world of Robotics, which results from the combination of the words "Domus" (house) and "Robotics".

Not only the industry benefit from new technologies, but so do our homes. Home Automation appears to automate every home, bringing several advantages, among which include: more energy savings, easier to control the brightness of a room, controlled consumption in a room and more security.

Due to these factors, I found a new project idea.

Firstly, I conducted intense research on what could I control in my home and start with two smart plugs. Secondly, I continued my research in search of a free “open source” platform to use, experimenting with OpenHAB and Hass.io (having focused on the second mentioned). Finally, I prepared the outlets and configured them in the system. Additionally, at each room, I can see the flow of energy consumed and the hours they were connected.

RC Car controlled by a smartphone

Explanation

Since a young age I have loved motorsports and watching documentaries about vehicles (normal cars, F1 cars, airplanes, aircraft, etc.), so I decided to make "a car from scratch".

To start I needed a remote controlled car chassis and its engines in order to control the car. After that, I bought the needed elements to build the vehicle, including: 1 L298N, 1 HC-05, 1 Buzzer, 1 HC-SR04, 1 9v Battery Connector, 1 Arduino Nano. Then, I put it all together, programmed the Arduino and bring my first car to life.

RC CAR Features:

- Bluetooth (HC-05) to control via any Android smartphone;

- Sensor (HC-SR04) used to control how far the car is from the nearest object. The buzzer activates if the object is 30 cm away, thus causing a sound. The sound increases from 10cm to 10cm. If the car is 1 cm from the object, it stops.

Controlling LEDs and Temperature & Humidity Sensor

Explanation

LED1 switches on at 17h o’clock and switches off at 20h o’clock. Adding to this, we can turn it on by an IP address.

LED2 is the red and allows me to change the brightness.

Temperature and humidity measurements are read from DHT22 sensor.
